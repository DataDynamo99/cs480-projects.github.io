Toss My Salad

Recipe Helper For All Of Your Waiting Ingredients.

Introduction

Welcome to Toss My Salad! Use this tool to find recipes for yourself to try at home. Take your spare ingredients in your kitchen and discover the variety of meals that can be created. Perfect for beginner cooks who don't know where to start with all of their random ingredients.

About Us

We are Team JJM. Created by James Avila, Joshua Reyes, and Michael Yaskowitz.

How It Works

To begin, follow the link below and create an account. Once the account is created, you can use the search bar to input your ingredients to begin searching for recipes to try for yourself.
