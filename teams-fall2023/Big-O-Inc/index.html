CPP Finder Keeper

We are a web service specializing in creating an interactive campus map and course scheduler for Cal Poly Pomona. With Cal Poly being such a large campus, it is very difficult to navigate and understand the time management it takes to walk to certain classes on time. Our goal with this web service is to provide two things: a convient tool for students to plan out their course schedules and to simplify campus navigation, especially for newer students, to make it easier to navigate between classes.
